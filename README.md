# An ontology house of cards

This repository represents a house-of-cards-level new metaphysical ontology.
As far as I know, this ontology is novel,
a new combination of several levels of explanation of what the world is or might be.

If you want to take speculative metaphysics, contemplative phenomenology, and anomalous experiences seriously,
without dropping standards of structural clarity, feel free to proceed.
If you are stuck to materialism or dismiss research on contemplative or anomalous experiences, it is better to leave.

In a way, I try to span the whole stretch from
quantum physics ([QFT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_field_theory)) to
[Advaita Vedānta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Advaita_Vedanta),
[Kashmir Shaivism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kashmir_Shaivism) and
[Dzogchen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dzogchen),
aiming to integrate such thinkers as
[Whitehead](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alfred_North_Whitehead),
[Lewis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Lewis_(philosopher)),
[Velmans](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Max_Velmans),
[Kastrup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bernardo_Kastrup),
[Faggin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federico_Faggin), and
[Vervaeke](https://www.uc.utoronto.ca/staff-faculty-profile/john-vervaeke),
together with interpretations of physics by
[Everett](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hugh_Everett_III),
[Griffiths](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Griffiths_(physicist)),
[Hartle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Hartle), and
[Zurek](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wojciech_H._Zurek) in one hand and e.g.
[Barbour](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Julian_Barbour) and
[D'Ariano](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giacomo_Mauro_D%27Ariano) in another hand,
and then add some emphasis on understanding trauma, as expressed e.g. by
[Levine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Somatic_experiencing#Peter_Levine),
[van der Kolk](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bessel_van_der_Kolk),
[Maté](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gabor_Maté), and
[Hübl](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas_Hübl).

A particular twist here is to try to provide a structural ontology that is compatible
with some unusual phenomena, such as
[NDE perception](https://med.virginia.edu/perceptual-studies/our-research/near-death-experiences-ndes/),
[past life memories](https://med.virginia.edu/perceptual-studies/our-research/children-who-report-memories-of-previous-lives/),
some time perception anomalies, and
so called [enlightenment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enlightenment_in_Buddhism).

In one level, this project is completely foolish.  It is unrealistic for one person to make such a synthesis.
At another level, I seem to make some initial progress, largely thanks to the recent advances of AI.
